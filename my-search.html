Update Your Search Criteria

Use this page to submit updated inputs for an active search. Your original brief will be recalibrated and three refreshed Google Docs delivered to your inbox within minutes.

When to use this

Your hiring manager has changed priorities or added new requirements since your original brief

You've received candidate feedback that sharpens what you're actually looking for

You have new ideal candidate profiles to add that will sharpen your Boolean strings and target companies

Your search has stalled and you want to recalibrate your strategy with fresh inputs

What you'll need

  • 1
    Your Search ID — found in the subject line of your original output delivery email. It looks like: Search ID: [unique code]
  • 2
    Updated inputs — revised job description, new hiring manager notes, additional candidate profiles, or any combination of the above
  • 3
    A note on what changed — a brief description of what's different helps the AI understand what to recalibrate vs. what to preserve from your original brief

How it works

1

Enter your Search ID

This connects your update to your original search so HireSignalIQ can combine your new inputs with your original brief.

2

Submit your updated inputs

Paste your new notes, revised JD, or additional candidate profiles. Only include what has changed — your original inputs are already on file.

3

Receive your updated brief

Three refreshed Google Docs arrive in your inbox within minutes — Search Brief, Screening Kit, and Rewritten JD — with a summary of what changed.

Can't find your Search ID? Check the subject line of your original output delivery email — it will read: HireSignalIQ Search Brief — [Role] | Search ID: [code]. If you can't locate it, email us at hello@hiresignaliq.ai and we'll look it up for you.
