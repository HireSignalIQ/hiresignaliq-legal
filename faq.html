General

HireSignalIQ is an AI-powered recruiting intelligence platform that serves both sides of the hiring equation simultaneously. Recruiters use it to generate complete search briefs, Boolean strings, screening kits, and rewritten job descriptions in minutes. Job seekers use it to receive a full application brief — including a HireSignal Fit Score, ATS-optimized resume bullets, cover letter, LinkedIn optimization, and networking strategy — all tailored to a specific role. It is the only platform in the market built by a practicing senior recruiter that serves both audiences from one AI engine.
HireSignalIQ was built by Gary Bresien, a senior executive search practitioner with 25 years of experience running searches across tech firms, AI startups, cybersecurity companies, and executive leadership roles. Every prompt, every output section, and every feature reflects real recruiting expertise — not keyword-matching algorithms built by engineers who have never made a hire. The AI thinks like a headhunter because it was built by one.
Three steps. First, choose your path — recruiter or job seeker — and pay securely via Stripe. You are immediately redirected to your intake form. Second, submit your inputs: paste your job description, resume, LinkedIn profile, hiring manager notes, or candidate profiles depending on which product you are using. All optional fields are clearly marked. Third, receive your output: a complete, formatted Google Doc (or multiple docs for recruiters) arrives in your inbox within minutes, alongside links to your personal Google Drive folder and application tracker.
Most outputs are delivered within 2 to 5 minutes of submitting your intake form. Recruiter briefs, which include three separate Google Docs, typically take slightly longer than job seeker briefs. You will receive an email with direct links to all your documents as soon as they are ready.
Your submission data is processed through OpenAI's API and stored in your personal Google Drive folder. HireSignalIQ does not sell your data to third parties. Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe, which is PCI-compliant — HireSignalIQ never sees or stores your payment information. Your Google Drive folder is private to you and accessible only via the link sent to your email.

For Recruiters

The Search Brief is a comprehensive Google Doc that includes: a role summary and what the hiring manager actually wants (beyond what the JD says), must-have and nice-to-have competencies, target companies and adjacent talent pools, Boolean search strings ready for LinkedIn Recruiter, a LinkedIn Recruiter search recipe with three targeted searches and InMail intelligence, compensation intelligence and market context, red flags to screen for, and a recommended search timeline. If you submit ideal candidate profiles, every section is calibrated against those real-world examples.
Intake Call Intelligence is a feature unique to HireSignalIQ. When you paste your hiring manager call notes or a meeting transcript into the intake form, the AI extracts the signals that a job description never captures — unstated preferences, cultural fit requirements, red flags the hiring manager mentioned verbally, team dynamics, and the real reasons previous hires did not work out. This produces a significantly sharper search brief than one built from the job description alone.
You can submit up to three ideal candidate examples — LinkedIn profiles or resumes — alongside your job description. The AI identifies patterns across their backgrounds: where they worked, what their career trajectory looked like, which skills they share, and which companies they came from. It then uses those patterns to sharpen your Boolean strings, refine your target company list, and calibrate your competency definitions against real-world examples rather than generic descriptions.
Update Search Criteria is included with every Recruiter plan subscription at no additional cost. It allows you to submit new inputs to an existing search using your Search ID — the unique identifier delivered in your original output email. You can add new candidate profiles, revised requirements, updated intake notes, or any other new information. HireSignalIQ combines your original inputs with the new ones and produces three fully refreshed Google Docs. You can use it at hiresignaliq.ai/my-search.
Enterprise platforms like Eightfold AI, SeekOut, and hireEZ are built for large organizations with dedicated HR technology teams. They require complex implementations, annual contracts ranging from $30,000 to $200,000, and months of onboarding. HireSignalIQ is built specifically for boutique recruiting firms and independent search professionals who need the output quality of a fully staffed search firm without the overhead. At $49 per month with no contract and output in minutes, it serves a completely different buyer with a completely different set of needs.

For Job Seekers

The HireSignal Score is a fit percentage — scored out of 100 — that tells you how well your background matches a specific role before you apply. It is calculated by analyzing your resume and LinkedIn profile against the full job description, evaluating your match across skills, experience level, industry background, and keyword alignment. The score also shows you exactly where you are strong and where you have gaps, so you can decide whether to apply, how to position yourself, and what to address in your cover letter.
Your Application Brief is a complete Google Doc that includes: your HireSignal Score with a breakdown of strengths and gaps, ATS keyword analysis showing which keywords are present and missing from your resume, tailored resume bullets written in your voice for the specific role, a professional summary rewrite, a three-paragraph cover letter that sounds like you rather than a generic AI output, LinkedIn profile optimization including headline rewrites and skills to feature, a targeted networking plan, and a clear application recommendation — including whether to apply and how to approach the search strategically.
Teal, Jobright, and Careerflow are built by engineers and serve only job seekers. HireSignalIQ was built by a senior recruiter and serves both recruiters and job seekers. This matters because the outputs think like a headhunter — the AI understands what recruiters are actually looking for, not just what ATS systems scan for. HireSignalIQ also uses simple monthly pricing with no weekly billing traps. Teal charges $13 per week — effectively $52 per month — and Jobright has faced significant user complaints about auto-renewal practices.
